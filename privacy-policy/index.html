Privacy Policy

Last updated:16.03.26

UK Trade Websites ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect any information you provide when using our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you contact us through our website:

  • Your name

  • Email address

  • Phone number

  • Business details you choose to provide

  • Any information submitted through our contact form

We only collect information necessary to respond to your enquiry or provide our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to enquiries

  • Provide website services

  • Communicate with you about your request

  • Improve our services

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. How We Store Your Information

Any personal information you provide is stored securely and only accessed by authorised individuals.

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry or provide our services.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience and analyse website traffic.

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help websites function effectively.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to help operate our website, such as:

  • website hosting providers

  • website analytics tools

These providers only process data necessary to perform their services.

6. Your Data Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you

  • Request correction of inaccurate information

  • Request deletion of your personal data

To make a request, please contact us using the details below.

7. Contact Us
